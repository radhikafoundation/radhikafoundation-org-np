About Us

Radhika Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting underprivileged, orphaned and vulnerable children across Nepal. We believe every child deserves quality education, dignity and opportunity.

Through educational support, welfare programs and community initiatives, we strive to create positive and lasting change in society.

Our Mission

To provide value-based modern education, protection, care and opportunities to disadvantaged children.

Our Vision

A society where every child has equal access to education, safety, dignity and opportunity.

Our Goal

Building a brighter future through education, compassion and empowerment.
